About Eiichiro Oda

Eiichiro Oda is a Japanese manga artist, best known for his world-renowned manga series *One Piece*. Born on January 1, 1975, in Kumamoto, Japan, Oda became passionate about drawing manga at a young age. By the time he was 22 years old, *One Piece* debuted in Shueisha's Weekly Sh≈çnen Jump magazine in 1997.

*One Piece* has since become one of the best-selling manga series of all time, with over 490 million copies in circulation worldwide. Oda's incredible world-building, compelling characters, and imaginative storytelling have garnered millions of fans across the globe.

Major Achievements

  • Creator of the long-running and best-selling manga series *One Piece*
  • Winner of the Grand Prize at the 41st Japan Cartoonists Association Awards
  • Holds the Guinness World Record for the most copies published for the same comic series by a single author
  • Authored over 1,000 chapters of *One Piece* and counting
